Online References for Sources Cited in the Book

These references belong to the book Among Digitized Manuscripts by L.W. Cornelis van Lit. Look at the landing page or see the live repository for more information or contact the author.

Only the DOI standard has been included. Other proprietary standards exist (such as run by arXiv, Jstor or the URN-Service der deutschen nationalbibliothek) and other hosting services exist (such as archive.org or university repositories) but they have not been included. Nearly all sources mentioned in this book can be found online either as a born-digital document or a digitized document, but the precarious state of the digital world is noticeable even among these sources: websites I used at the beginning of this project no longer exist.
